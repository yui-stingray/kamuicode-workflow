The base prompt "A cat playing under a beautiful cherry blossom tree" was analyzed to create two distinct, optimized prompts. For the image prompt (Imagen4), the focus is on static, high-detail visual elements: a photorealistic kitten, intricate petal details, soft lighting, and a sharp focus to capture a single perfect moment. For the video prompt (Hailuo-02), the emphasis is on dynamic action and flow: the kitten chasing falling petals, slow-motion pouncing, and smooth camera panning to create a sense of gentle, continuous movement.
